Critical Insight
This isn't "flexibility"—it's a governance crisis. Every team maintains metadata differently, creating blind spots in lineage, classification, and access control across our entire data ecosystem.

Critical Pain Points Today

  • Metadata Maelstrom: No single source of truth for data lineage or PHI/PII classification. Tracing a HEDIS measure's origin takes weeks—and often fails.
  • Governance Gaps: Inconsistent access controls and audit trails across tools create HIPAA compliance risks. One misconfigured dbt model could leak PHI.
  • Operational Tax: Teams waste 40% effort on undifferentiated heavy lifting: rebuilding broken pipelines, reconciling metric discrepancies, debugging cross-tool failures.
  • Business Impact: Risk Adjustment & Quality Measures delayed due to untrusted data. New value-based care initiatives stall waiting 6+ months for integrated data.

Addressing Concerns: "But We Already Use Fabric for Power BI..."

Strategic Insight

Limiting Fabric to Power BI is like buying a Ferrari to drive 25mph in parking lots. We're missing the engine that powers the entire data lifecycle.

Practical Path Forward

1. Start with high-impact pilot (Quality/Stars Mart)
2. Integrate Snowflake via "Mirrored Warehouse"
3. Apply Fabric governance to existing assets
4. Scale proven patterns across enterprise

Mitigating Risks
• HIPAA-compliant out of the box
• Native Purview integration leverages existing investment
• Open formats (Parquet, Delta) prevent lock-in
• Microsoft SLAs for healthcare workloads
• Coexistence strategy: Fabric for governance, Snowflake for scale

The Stakes: Why This Can't Wait
